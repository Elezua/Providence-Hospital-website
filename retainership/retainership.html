Retainership Package Options

Flexible Model

Pay-As-You-Go (PAYG)

Ideal for individuals and organizations that prefer a flexible, usage-based arrangement. Clients enjoy discounted rates, priority scheduling, and guaranteed access to care, while paying only for services rendered. No upfront bulk payments.

Best suited for: Organizations or individuals with low to moderate, unpredictable healthcare utilization who want cost control.

Select PAYG
Premium Model

Prepaid: Providence Health Credit Plan

Operates on an annual subscription basis, where a fixed amount is paid upfront to cover a defined scope of medical services. Prepaid clients enjoy comprehensive access with minimal or no extra charges at the point of care.

Best suited for: Corporates seeking predictable annual healthcare budgeting, and families who want comprehensive coverage and cashless access.

Key Terms & Conditions

Eligibility & Duration expand_more

• Open to individuals, families, and registered corporate organizations. Dependents may be added subject to fees.

• Prepaid plans run for a fixed 12-month period. PAYG registration is valid for 12 months and renewable annually.

• Renewal notices are issued at least 30 days before expiry; rates may be reviewed at renewal.

Payment Terms & Exclusions expand_more

• Prepaid premiums are due in full prior to activation. PAYG clients settle invoices within 7–14 days of billing.

• Exclusions include: pre-existing chronic conditions not declared, cosmetic/elective procedures, fertility treatment, organ transplantation, and chronic dialysis (unless separately contracted).

Limits, Authorization & Termination expand_more

• Each plan tier carries a defined annual limit. Procedures above a specified cost threshold require pre-authorization.

• Either party may terminate the agreement with 30 days' written notice. Prepaid premiums are non-refundable after 30 days from activation, except on a pro-rated basis where explicitly agreed.

• All client medical data is handled in strict confidence in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA).

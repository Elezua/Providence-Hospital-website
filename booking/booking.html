Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our registration process

How much is the registration fee?

expand_more
Registration fees is 5000 Naira only. You'll see the exact amount on the Paystack payment page before completing your transaction. Note that this fee is non-refundable, and doess not cover for initial or specialist consultation.

How long does it take to get a confirmation?

expand_more
For self-pay patients, confirmation is usually within a few hours. For HMO/insurance patients, verification may take 2 to 3 hours depending on your provider's response time.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more
Through Paystack, we accept all major debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, and USSD payments. All transactions are 100% secure.

How do I schedule an appointment after registration?

expand_more
Simple! after your registration is confirmed, our team will contact you to schedule an appointment at your preferred time depending on the specialist's availability. You can also reach out to us directly via WhatsApp or phone for scheduling assistance.
